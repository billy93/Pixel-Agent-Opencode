OVERVIEW
--------
Claude Opus 4.6 is Anthropic's most capable AI model as of February 2026. It's a hybrid 
reasoning model designed for the most demanding professional tasks including software 
engineering, AI agents, and enterprise workflows.

HYBRID REASONING / EXTENDED THINKING MODE
------------------------------------------
Opus 4.6 offers two modes:
1. Instant responses for quick tasks
2. Extended thinking for complex reasoning

Extended Thinking Features:
- Fine-grained controls for adjusting effort level
- Balance between performance, latency, and cost
- Thinking summaries help understand reasoning process
- Best for: Complex reasoning tasks, extended agentic work, multi-step coding 
  projects, deep research

When to Use Extended Thinking:
- Performance and accuracy matter more than latency
- Complex reasoning tasks
- Extended agentic work
- Multi-step coding projects
- Deep research
